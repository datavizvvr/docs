Question: What is a Chapter?
Answer: A chapter is a Local Unit of BJS, formed by a group of Jain Community members living in a particular village or city to collectively contribute towards the aim of Nation Building through holistic development of the society.
Question: What is Registered Chapter?
Answer: Registered Chapter a legal entity, registered under Societies Registration Act 1860. In Maharashtra, Gujarat & Madhya Pradesh, chapters can be registered under Societies Registration Act as well as Public Trust Act.
Question: What is Affiliated Chapters?
Answer: Affiliated Chapter of BJS is a Registered Chapter under Societies Registration Act 1860 along with affiliation consent letter from BJS-HO. Affiliated Chapters will take initiatives towards Nation Building under the guidance of BJS-HO. Affiliated Chapters must be aligned with the Objectives of BJS-HO.
